Important Notes

This is a simplified demonstration of the claimed Torah codes. The actual analysis requires:

  • Precise Hebrew manuscripts with exact letter counts
  • Consistent spacing and letter intervals
  • Academic verification of claimed patterns
  • Statistical analysis to determine if patterns exceed random chance

Many scholars remain skeptical of Bible codes as a legitimate phenomenon.
